Frequently Asked Questions

What types of dumpsters do you offer?

We offer a variety of dumpsters, including roll-off containers, commercial dumpsters, and residential dumpsters in multiple sizes to accommodate different project needs.

What types of materials can be disposed of?

Our roll-off dumpsters are suitable for various materials, including construction debris, household waste, and yard waste. However, hazardous materials, such as chemicals and electronics, are prohibited.

How can I use a dumpster for home renovations?

Our dumpsters are perfect for disposing of debris and construction waste during remodeling projects. Simply fill the dumpster with your renovation waste, and we'll handle the rest.

How do I schedule a dumpster rental?

You can schedule a rental by calling us directly at (901) 205-9256 or by filling out our online booking form on our website.

What areas do you serve?

We serve a wide range of areas including both residential and commercial locations. Please check our service area map or contact us to confirm if we cover your location.

What is the rental period for a dumpster?

Our standard rental period is 7 days. If you need the dumpster for a longer period, we can arrange extended rentals at an additional cost.

How much does it cost to rent a dumpster?

Pricing varies depending on the dumpster's size and the rental period's length. Contact us for a detailed quote.

Do I need a permit to place a dumpster on my property?

Permits may be required depending on your location and where you plan to place the dumpster. We can help guide you through the permit process.

How soon can I get a dumpster delivered?

We strive to deliver dumpsters within 24-48 hours of your order, depending on availability and your location.

Can I place the dumpster on the street?

Placement on the street may require a permit from your local municipality. We can assist with obtaining the necessary permits.

How do I prepare my site for dumpster delivery?

Ensure the delivery area is clear of obstacles, and there is enough space for the truck to maneuver. The ground should be level and stable.

Can I move the dumpster once it’s placed?

Dumpsters should not be moved once placed. If repositioning is necessary, please contact us to arrange for it.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept various forms of payment. Payment details will be provided when you book your rental.

How does MainLine Containers manage waste disposal for its dumpster rentals in Memphis, TN?

We prioritize environmentally responsible waste disposal. Our team ensures all waste is taken to authorized facilities, complying with local regulations and sustainability practices.

What happens if I need to cancel my dumpster rental?

If you need to cancel your rental, please notify us as soon as possible. Cancellations made within a certain timeframe may be eligible for a full or partial refund, depending on our cancellation policy.
